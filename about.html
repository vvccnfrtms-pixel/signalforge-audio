About SignalForge Audio

SignalForge Audio creates boutique guitar pedals for musicians who demand premium tone and unmatched reliability. Based in East London, South Africa, each pedal is hand-tested and assembled in small-batch runs to ensure quality, consistency, and exclusivity.

We collaborate with artists and engineers to develop innovative sounds, limited edition finishes, and versatile effects that excel in both studio and live performance.

Our Philosophy

Every pedal is designed with the musician in mind. SignalForge Audio combines artistry, technology, and craftsmanship to produce instruments that inspire creativity.

Small-Batch Excellence

Limited production ensures attention to detail, higher quality control, and products that retain their value and exclusivity over time.

Global Reach

While handcrafted locally, SignalForge Audio pedals are shipped worldwide, ensuring musicians everywhere can experience premium tone.
